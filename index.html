invisible.js


Invisible.js Makes Stuff Invisible™ by transforming your input into Binary1 with a Twist.

By substituting2 a space and tab character with the resulting 1s and 0s we can achieve invisibility.

Try it out on the Hackers Manifesto !

1. Literally meaning "Composed of two pieces"
2. Change C1, C2 and END to remap the defaults.
